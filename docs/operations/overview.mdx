# Operations and Maintenance

O&M modules support post-handover operations teams with structured asset and maintenance workflows.

## Asset register

- Create and maintain project assets
- Track serials, categories, warranty dates, and locations
- Attach documents and drawings

## Maintenance

- Define maintenance schedules by frequency
- Generate work orders from due tasks
- Mark schedule completion and update next due cycles

## Inspections

- Build inspection templates
- Run inspections and capture results
- Complete/sign off runs
- Generate PDF inspection reports

## Handover and FM dashboard

- Maintain handover summaries/briefs
- Track FM KPIs and status rollups
- Export key O&M datasets as CSV

## Plan requirements

Operations features are plan-gated. If unavailable, verify your workspace billing plan and entitlement settings.
