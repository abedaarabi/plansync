# Getting Started

Use this checklist to launch PlanSync with your team.

## 1) Create or join a workspace

- Sign in through your PlanSync app URL
- Create a workspace or accept an invite link
- Confirm your email when prompted

## 2) Create your first project

In the workspace, create a project and set:

- Project name and location
- Currency and measurement system
- Stage and delivery dates
- Operations mode (if your team uses O&M features)

## 3) Add team members

Invite internal and external collaborators:

- `SUPER_ADMIN`: full workspace administration
- `ADMIN`: project/workspace management
- `MEMBER`: standard project access

For scoped access, assign members to specific projects.

## 4) Upload drawings and files

- Create folder structures or apply templates
- Upload sheets/documents
- Open files in the viewer and start markup/collaboration

## 5) Enable the right modules

Based on your plan and project setup, turn on modules your team needs:

- Issues
- RFIs
- Punch lists
- Field reports
- Takeoff
- Proposals
- O&M (assets, maintenance, inspections, occupant workflows)

## 6) Invite clients to portal views

When ready, share project data using client and proposal portals, and occupant reporting links where applicable.

Continue with [Workspaces and Projects](workspaces-and-projects) for setup details.
