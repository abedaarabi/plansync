# Takeoff and Materials

PlanSync ties quantity takeoff directly to drawing versions and workspace material libraries.

## Takeoff lines

Track scoped quantities across sheets and revisions:

- List takeoff lines by file version or project
- Create/update/remove lines
- Bulk operations for efficiency
- Snapshot and sync workflows for repeatable calculations

## Revision awareness

Takeoff lines can be compared against newer drawing revisions so teams can quickly identify quantity drift.

## Materials catalog

Workspace admins can maintain reusable material data:

- Categories and item definitions
- Units, unit rates, and currencies
- Custom attributes and templates
- CSV import/update workflows

## Why this matters

A shared material catalog keeps estimate assumptions consistent across projects and proposal cycles.
